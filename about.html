About PCN

Education

I received a Bsc in science from University College Dublin and I hold a MSc in Nutrition from University College Cork. Upon graduating from my Masters I worked under the guidance of Emma Tester, the performance Nutritionist for Munster Rugby.

Sports Background

I have a keen interest in most sports but my specialty would be in GAA. I played at a competitive level for several years, playing senior football for the St.Finbarr’s club in Cork City. I have a deep understanding from my academic, coaching and playing roles, in the benefits nutrition has on elite performance. I have experience working with many elite athletes and sports organisations, and I am currently the ‘Performance Nutritionist Coach’ for the Cork minor footballers and hurlers.

Goal

PCN’s goal is to educate people on the fundamentals of nutrition. I want to empower my clients to make sustained changes to their diet that leads to long last benefits to their health or sports performance PCN is about educating the consumer about nutrition, so overtime they are able to make better more informed food choices in order to achieve their health or performance goals. It’s a slow and steady approach, about sticking to basic meal plans at the beginning until over a period of time, the consumer is able to make food choices off their own bat.
